production campaign.

Notes
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- All samples are designed for the OMTF overlap acceptance, roughly |eta| in
  the 0.8 to 1.24 band for the overlap-focused multi-muon samples.
- The prompt single-muon and displaced single-muon samples use full phi to
  avoid imposing processor-window bias.
- The same-window samples restrict phi intentionally to ensure multiple muons
  occupy the same OMTF processor region.
- The NanoAOD output contains OMTF candidates, propagated gen muons, and GMT
  stub tables; the omtf_hits output contains the OMTF-region input stub data
  needed for ML training and full replacement studies.
